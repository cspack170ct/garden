Garden Plot

How We Manage the Plot

This year (2025), the pack has its first garden at the Kolp Community Garden Plots on Meadow Rd.

The plot is free and open to anyone in the pack who wants to be involved! If you have something you want to plant, feel free to stake a claim! Just take a picture of where you've planted and send it the pack email along with a description of what it is so we can update the map.

We are going to have a water barrel, watering cans, and gardening tools available at the plot.

The Farmington Food Pantry accepts any extra produce that we grow.

Coordination

We have a Google Group to let everyone share information about the garden with the group. Email the pack to be added to it.

We also have a sign up if you are available to weed, water, or harvest so we don't duplicate efforts.
